about me

25 years old with a keen eye for detail and
all things visual.

I create fabulous visuals and user experiences.
I aim to dazzle through elegant artistic designs that surprise by playfully discarding the boring and conventional. Without compromising functionality, I want to embody beautiful simplicity in my designs.

In association with our content marketing project we were to re-brand and redesign a beverage of our own choosing. My partner and I chose the tea brand, Lipton Tea.

Our focus lied on the enjoyment people have together when drinking tea. We wanted to go for a more personal approach, where a modern design were created with twists of hand-drawn elements.

The rebranding came with new key values such as happiness, closeness and sustainability.
